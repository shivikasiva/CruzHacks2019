Simply The Best Student App Out There.

Students can use Sluggish to plan their schedule, keep daily reminders, stay fit, check weather, manage sleep schedule, eat healthy and switch the dorm light on or off and even open or close the blinds.

Simplicity.

Easy to guide through the app and the various resources it provides. Simple click of a button and basic input options.

Resourceful.

All of your needs in one place. Sluggish brings together everything about student life that you need to keep track of and gives you the tools you need to make things work smoothly and stress-free.

Purposeful.

Sluggish strives to seamlessly remove stress from student life. We continue to improve our technology and add new features to make your life as easy as easy as you deserve.

Smart

Helps you accomplish academic and everyday dorm life tasks. IoT integration allows for students to use the app in order to turn light switch on or off and open or close blinds.

How The App Works?

Sign-Up

Sign Up or Register to have a personal profile and dashboard that you can return to every time.

Create

Setup your schedule, daily and weekly activities and personal preferences.

Compose

Get daily and weekly reminders, suggestion on healthy sleep and eating habits and fitness motivation.

IoT

Internet of Things integration will allow you to automate light switch and window blinds operations.

Loaded With Features You Would Absolutely Love.

We have UNLIMITED features personalized to YOUR specific needs.

Planner

Student Planner with class schedule, daily before and after class reminders, homework management, study plans, activities, projects, and any extracurriculars.

Fitness

Exercise plans, fitness updates, gym settings, healthy food and sleep suggestions, personalized fitness data storage, etc.

Internet of Things

Light on and off switch options, Blinds open and close options, personalized voice controlled devices, etc.

Food

Food sharing options, dining hall timings, pantry availabilities, left over food donation, etc. Includes payment option within the app including ability to share flexi dollars or pay each other through cash or credit.

Social Media

Connects students all across campus based on similar interests, related classes, club involvement, college affiliation, etc. Also helps one make new friends and meet up with students for social events for good such as hiking, volunteering, etc.

Safety

Allows users to update their current time location and share it with friends and family. Also allows for shortcut options to call for help or report danger to the law enforcement. Update community regarding sudden problems or ask for potential help from fellow community members.
